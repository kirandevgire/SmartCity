Fashion Street

Pune’s fashion street, much like the one we have in Mumbai, has a wide array of fashion stalls of clothing and accessories to choose from. All you fashionistas, the name says it all. The latest, trendy fashion can be found here. Be it a different sling bag for every new dress, or a cute pair of PJs for a sleepover at your bestie’s – you must visit Fashion Street. And yes, just like FC Road, Camp has a lot of eateries to take care of your ‘after shopping hunger pangs’.

FC Road

Apart from the restaurants, why do you think this place is so hyped? Located in heart of the city, FC Road has local shops that sell trendy footwear and bags. If buying in bulk is what matters to you, FC Road is a must visit. Many vendors come in the evenings with their cart and sell tops, for as cheap as INR 150. Though not very long lasting, they can definitely be used for a few months. Other than shopping, the restaurants on FC Road always welcome you when you wanna grab a quick bite. Tip: Avoid weekends, if you hate rush.

Phoenix Market City

It is without a doubt, the biggest shopping complex in the city of Pune. Feel like blowing up some big bucks? This is where you should be! The fact that this is the biggest mall in Pune is itself the biggest reason which attracts thousands of shoppers throughout the year. A decent looking place, Phoenix Market City offers a great experience for those who are looking for quality dine-in options. At the place, you can spend a long time as it has many options which cannot allow you to get bored. From tasty food to kid zones, theatres to different branded stores, the place has almost everything to offer. A much-recommended place if you are planning a trip to Pune.

A Shopaholic’s Guide to Pune

The city of Pune is flourishing as a shopper’s paradise. With its fashion market expanding with each passing day, the city now has everything from designer boutiques and high street brand stores, to garment retailers and tiny little clothing and accessory stalls.

Mahatma gandhi Road

Referred to as M.G. Road by the locals, this strip is a common destination for most shoppers. From the bigger stores to small and tiny stalls, the strip has enough to satiate the shopaholic in you! (Clovers Centre- with over 300 stores, it’s a definite pick on our list). In addition, it also has a number of food stalls to satisfy those hunger pangs.

Hong Kong Lane

Located near Garware Bridge, Hong Kong Lane is also a must-visit shopping center where you can get all the items like Footwear for men and women, attractive dresses, different accessories like bag and jewelry, books and much more. This is a place where you can roam around comfortably for hours and can fill your bags without rushing. So plan a visit to this market while traveling to Pune for a fine shopping experience.

Seasons Mall

At seasons Mall you can have one of the finest shopping experiences in Pune. From dining to entertaining the place has everything for the visitors. If you are a foodie, there are a number of restaurants from where you can have a finger licking food. Apart from that, there are many stores for youngsters, adults, and kids from where they can shop for their needs. A perfect place to enjoy quality time with family and friends.
